BITSTREAM FORMAT
----------------
For rate 6.4 kbit/s, the bitstreamfile contains for each 10 ms speech
frame, 66 16-bit words.
The first word is the syncword SYNC_WORD
The second word is the framesize contains the value 64.

For SID frame, the bitstreamfile contains for each 10 ms speech
frame, 17 (or 18 )16-bit words.
The first word is the syncword SYNC_WORD
The second word is the framesize contains the value 15 (or 16).

the next 15 (or 16) words contain the following parameters:
001      LPC1-   SID MA predictor switch
002      LPC1-   SID Lsp : 1st stage 5 bit
003      LPC1-
004      LPC1-
005      LPC1-
006      LPC1-
007      LPC2-   SID Lsp : 2nd stage 4 bit
008      LPC2-
009      LPC2-
010      LPC2-
011      GAIN-  SID gain : 5 bit
012      GAIN-
013      GAIN-
014      GAIN-
015      GAIN-
(016     BIT_0)
